I found out my roommate has been stealing my food for 6 months and I only just caught him.

I've been living with this guy for about a year. Nice enough, keeps to himself, pays rent on time. But for the past 6 months I kept noticing my groceries disappearing faster than they should. I thought I was just eating more than I realized.

Last week I bought a very specific thing — a limited edition cereal that's not sold anywhere near us. I ordered it online as a treat for myself. Came home from work on Wednesday and it was already half gone. I hadn't touched it.

I set up a cheap camera pointed at the kitchen. Watched the footage yesterday. There he was, at 2am, helping himself to my cereal, my leftovers, my snacks. Just casually standing there eating my food like it was nothing.

I confronted him this morning. He said he thought we were "sharing." We never had that conversation. Ever. He got defensive and said I was overreacting. I told him he owes me for 6 months of groceries or he needs to find somewhere else to live.

He called me petty. I called my landlord. The landlord is on my side but says it's complicated to remove him mid-lease.

I don't even know what to do now. I'm furious but also just exhausted.
